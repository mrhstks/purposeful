Who Am I???

Born in Manhatten, New York, I have managed to live a very colorful life during my 25 years on earth. As a child, I lived in several states, but I spent most of my life in the state of Virginia. This kind of movement exposed me to so many kinds of ideas and people; it was here that began to develop a love for the creative arts. I've been exposed to so many amazing experiences, both professionally and exclusively as a hobby. Yet, some of my greatest accomplishments include: marrying the man of my dreams, and the birth of our daughter. Along with my relationship with GOD, those two life events have had the greatest impact on how I view the world.

My goal as a wife, a mom, a creative, and a human is to Love GOD, Love Others, and Love Myself... and if something artistic happens then that's cool too
